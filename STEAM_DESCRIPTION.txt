[h1]Peace Deal Mechanics[/h1]

Peace Deal Mechanics adds negotiated mid-war settlements to Hearts of Iron IV without waiting for total capitulation.

[b]What it does:[/b] this mod adds a custom peace-deal diplomatic action that lets countries settle wars early through limited terms instead of always fighting to a full peace conference.

[h2]Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] Mid-war peace deal diplomacy
[*] Demand terms or offer a negotiated settlement
[*] Take states, give states, puppet states, demilitarize, and liberate territory
[*] Optional war reparations and forced disarmament
[*] AI countries now use the system on their own with conservative target selection
[*] AI can make narrow puppet demands in clear cases without bloating the system
[*] Rebuilt AI acceptance so credible settlements are judged by pressure, terms, and political context
[*] Three small game rules for system availability, AI participation, and AI acceptance stance
[*] Vanilla conditional surrender is disabled while Peace Deal Mechanics is active
[*] Optional Ceasefire Mechanics integration so active ceasefires can be turned into permanent peace cleanly
[/list]

[h2]How it plays[/h2]
[list]
[*] Winning countries can press for a limited settlement without waiting for total collapse
[*] Losing countries can offer concessions to preserve part of their position
[*] Accepted deals end the war immediately and apply a truce
[/list]

[b]Version:[/b] 1.0.0
[b]HOI4:[/b] 1.17.4.1+
[b]DLC Required:[/b] None
